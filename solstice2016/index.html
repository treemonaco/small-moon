About the Summer Solstice Music Festival

Help raise money to find a cure for Cystic Fibrosis!

Buzz and Jrray (aka Kim and Jerry) have been hosting Summer Solstice since 1991 to benefit the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation.

Solstice is an all day / night family-friendly music festival. Come on out to see old friends and make new ones, support live local music and celebrate the start of summer!

Thanks to our musicians, volunteers, friends, and sponsors, we have raised over $88,000 for CFF during the 9 past years.

Help us break our goal!

Our goal is to break $100,000 raised for CFF by Solstice!

FAQs

  • What should I bring?
    • A smile!
    • Solstice is a BYOB event. It's a long day, bring plenty!
      Plan to bring a cooler full of drinks and food!
      • Drinks
      • Water
      • Snacks
      • Food
    • Designated driver!
    • Sunscreen, bug spray & shade tent.
    • Games, toys, frisbee, baggo, balls, etc.
    • Cash, for t-shirts, raffles, 50/50, water ice., etc.
    • Warm clothes (it can get cold at night) & rain gear if needed.
    • Blanket or chairs.
    • Camera
    • Other cool humans. Sorry, we're not permitted to have dogs.
    • Please bring trash bags and clean up! Leave only footprints!
  • When is Solstice?

    Summer Solstice takes place June 11th 2016 from 3pm - 10pm.

    It is a rain or shine event! Bring rain gear if needed!

  • What is it for?

    Solstice benefits the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation.

    We have raised over $88,000 for CFF during the past 9 years.

  • Who is playing?

    There will be several awesome local bands playing throughout the day.

    Checkout the Lineup for more info.

  • Where is it?

    The event is held at the ChesLen Preserve, a beautiful 1200 acre nature preserve, in Chester County PA.

    1199 Cannery Road
    Coatesville, PA 19320

    Google Map Directions

  • Will there be food?

    Solstice is a BYOB event.

    We do have some vendors who may sell food, but you should plan to bring your own food and beverages.
